How to draw good! (An Expert's Guide)

1. Go to Apple Store
2. Get iPad
3. Set up iPad
4. Have 5 dollars
5. Add 5 dollars to your Apple Account
6. Download ProCreate for iPad
7. Open app
8. Create a new project
9. Go to Settings > Preferences
10. MOVE THE TOOLBAR TO THE RIGHT SIDE IDK HOW YOU USE IT ON THE LEFT SIDE OMLLLL
11. Draw a circle for the head
12. Draw lines
13. Color in the lines
14. Save your project

You now have a fully complete drawing to show to the world! Have fun drawing!
